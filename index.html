Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

What We Store

NeoArcadeX primarily runs locally on your Mac. For trial/licensing and optional services, the following may be used:

  • Device ID generated and stored in Keychain (trial/license)
  • Trial/license status (cached locally, periodically verified)
  • ScreenScraper credentials (if you choose to enter them)
  • Email address (newsletter opt‑in only)

How We Use It

  • Verify trial and Pro activation
  • Fetch metadata and media (ScreenScraper and/or our media server)
  • Optional email updates if subscribed

Storage

Your library data stays on your Mac. Device ID and license status are stored in Keychain. We don’t sell or share your data. No telemetry is sent by default.
