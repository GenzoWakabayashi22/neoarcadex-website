Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about NeoArcadeX

NeoArcadeX requires macOS 11.0 (Big Sur) or later, with a minimum of 8 GB RAM (16 GB recommended). You'll need at least 500 MB of disk space for the application, plus additional space for your ROM collection and downloaded artwork. The app works on both Intel and Apple Silicon Macs, with optimizations for Apple's M-series chips.

NeoArcadeX supports a wide range of emulators including RetroArch (with all cores), MAME, Dolphin (GameCube/Wii), PCSX2 (PlayStation 2), PPSSPP (PSP), DeSmuME (Nintendo DS), and many more. The app can automatically detect installed emulators and allows you to configure custom emulator paths for any platform.

Importing ROMs is simple: go to File → Import Games, select your ROM folder, and choose the platform. NeoArcadeX will automatically scan for compatible files and download metadata and artwork from our games database. Supported formats include .zip, .7z, .iso, .bin/.cue, and platform-specific formats. You can also drag and drop ROM files directly into the app.

The free version of NeoArcadeX includes all core features: game library management, emulator integration, automatic metadata downloads, and basic customization. The Premium version (€5) unlocks advanced features like cloud backup, premium themes, advanced filtering options, batch operations, and priority support. Premium also helps support continued development of NeoArcadeX.

Join our vibrant community on Discord where you can get help, share your setup, and connect with other retro gaming enthusiasts. We also have an active forum for detailed discussions and troubleshooting. For bug reports and feature requests, visit our GitHub repository.
